I recently completed my PhD in Optics, Photonics and Image Processing at CPPM, defended January 29th, 2026. My thesis focused on modeling the instrumental response of the Euclid NISP instrument within the DISPERS project, combining Zernike polynomial analysis and deep learning to characterize optical aberrations from PSF images.

I am currently looking for a postdoc position in Canada or the United States in optics, computational imaging, or machine learning for science. Get in touch!
