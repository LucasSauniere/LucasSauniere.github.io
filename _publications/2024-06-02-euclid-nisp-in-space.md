This paper presents the in-orbit status and performance of the **NISP (Near Infrared Spectrometer and Photometer)** instrument aboard the [Euclid space telescope](https://www.esa.int/Science_Exploration/Space_Science/Euclid), launched by ESA in July 2023.

The NISP instrument provides both:
- **Photometry** in the near-infrared (Y, J, H bands)
- **Grism spectroscopy** for measuring galaxy redshifts across a wide area of the sky

This collaborative paper gathers results from the Euclid Consortium's NISP working groups and covers the full performance characterization conducted during the commissioning phase.

**Authors:** W. Gillard, S. Dusini, K. Jahnke, E. Prieto, N. Auricchio, R. Barbier, **L. Sauniere**, et al.
