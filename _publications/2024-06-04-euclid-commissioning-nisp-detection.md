This paper reports on the commissioning results of the **NISP signal detection chain** — the full readout electronics and detector system of Euclid's near-infrared instrument.

Key topics covered:
- **HgCdTe detector array** characterization (noise, dark current, linearity)
- **In-orbit calibration** procedures and results
- **Performance validation** against pre-launch requirements

These results demonstrate that the NISP instrument is operating as expected, paving the way for the Euclid mission's wide-field cosmological survey.
