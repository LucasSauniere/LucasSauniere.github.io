We present **Spread**, a Python framework designed for Point Spread Function (PSF) based optical aberration analysis. 
The framework addresses two key challenges in low-resolution optical instrumentation:
- **Estimation** of Zernike polynomial coefficients from observed PSFs using deep learning (CNNs)
- **Reconstruction** of PSFs from Zernike coefficient descriptions

The approach is validated on simulated and real data from the Euclid NISP instrument, demonstrating its potential for in-orbit calibration and performance monitoring of space telescopes.
