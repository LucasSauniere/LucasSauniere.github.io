Oral presentation and poster at **SPIE Astronomical Telescopes + Instrumentation 2024** — one of the largest international gatherings of optical engineers and astronomers.

We presented our approach combining Zernike polynomial decomposition and deep learning to estimate optical aberrations directly from PSF images of low-resolution space instruments, demonstrated on the Euclid NISP instrument.

**Authors:** L. Sauniere, W. Gillard, J. Zoubian
